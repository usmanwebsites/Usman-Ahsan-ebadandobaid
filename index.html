A Legacy of Friendship & Taste

Welcome to Usman, Ahsan, Ebad & Obaid Foods. Located in the heart of Pakistan, our restaurant is a symbol of brotherhood and culinary excellence.

It all started when four friends from Pakistan decided to bring their favorite homemade recipes to the world. Usman is the master of BBQ, Ahsan specializes in aromatic Rice dishes, Ebad brings the spicy Karahi perfection, and Obaid crafts the sweetest traditional desserts.

We do not serve just food; we serve the culture of Pakistan. Every grain of rice and every spice is locally sourced to ensure you get the true "Watan Ka Zaiqa".
